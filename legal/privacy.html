Privacy Policy

Updated: Jan 2026

At AgriQuiz ("we", "our"), we believe in cultivating knowledge, not harvesting your personal data.

1. No Data Collection

We do not require you to create an account, sign up, or provide any personal identification information (PII) such as your name, email, or phone number.

2. Local Storage

To enhance your experience, we use Local Storage on your device to save:

  • Quiz progress & scores.
  • Theme preferences (Dark/Light).

Note: This data remains 100% on your device and is never sent to our servers.

3. Ads & Cookies

We may display ads (e.g., Google AdSense) to keep this tool free. Vendors use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits.

  • Google uses advertising cookies for ad personalization.
  • Opt-out at Google Ad Settings.

4. Analytics

We use anonymous, aggregated analytics to track popular quiz topics. This data cannot identify individual users.

5. Contact Us

Questions? Contact the developer at hello@toolblaster.com or via ToolBlaster.com.
