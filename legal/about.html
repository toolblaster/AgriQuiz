About AgriQuiz

Updated: Jan 2026

Welcome to AgriQuiz, your comprehensive digital companion for mastering Agriculture GK and quiz questions. Designed for precision, speed, and exam readiness.

1. Why AgriQuiz?

AgriQuiz solves the problem of scattered study material. We replace outdated PDFs with a consolidated, interactive agriculture question bank suitable for ICAR, IBPS, and state-level competitive exams.

2. Purpose & Pedagogy

We leverage Active Recall to enhance memory retention. Unlike passive reading, our mock-test style questions force your brain to retrieve information, solidifying concepts in Agronomy, Horticulture, and Animal Husbandry.

3. Key Benefits

Maximize your study efficiency with features designed for aspirants:

  • Instant Feedback: Correct your mistakes in real-time.
  • Zero Distractions: No ads or login walls to slow you down.
  • Progress Tracking: Local storage saves your quiz history.

4. Target Exams

Tailored for students and professionals preparing for:

  • Central: IBPS AFO, ICAR JRF/SRF, NABARD, FCI, CCI.
  • State: ADO, ADA, RAEO, UPCATET, BHU PET, JET, MCAER.
  • Academic: BSc & MSc Agriculture semester revisions.

5. Syllabus Coverage

Our database covers the complete agricultural spectrum:

  • Core: Agronomy, Soil Science, Genetics & Plant Breeding.
  • Applied: Horticulture, Entomology, Pathology, Nematology.
  • Allied: Animal Husbandry, Dairy, Extension, Economics.

6. Technical Edge

A modern Progressive Web App (PWA) built for speed:

  • Bilingual: Questions in English & Hindi.
  • Privacy: No data collection; 100% client-side.
